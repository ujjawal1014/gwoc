About Us

Mudberry Studio is a creative haven specializing in pottery design and workshops.

Welcome to Mudberry Studio, where passion meets clay and creativity flows endlessly!

At Mudberry Studio, our journey began with a simple idea: to create a space where artistry and pottery intertwine to form unique, awe-inspiring creations. Founded by Jaydeep Kotadiya, our studio has evolved into a haven for individuals seeking an outlet for their creativity through the medium of pottery.
